THE BOYZ (Korean: 더보이즈; formerly known as Cre.kerz) is a South Korean boy group formed by Cre.ker Entertainment in 2017. Originally with 12 members, they debuted on December 6, 2017 with the song 'Boy' from their mini-album 'THE FIRST'.

They have released 1 Studio Album, 5 Extended Plays (1 being Japanese) and 2 Single Albuns. They had their first fanmeeting tour named 'THE CASTLE' on January 2019. THE BOYZ won 8 Rookie Awards, attracting a lot of attention from the public.

They were a contestant on Mnet's Road to Kingdom, a show for not very well known korean boygroups to get the chance to shine and win a spot to compete on Kingdom (the main show like it's predecessor Queendom). THE BOYZ was the final winner with a total of 95,765 points.
